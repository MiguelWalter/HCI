HCI Resources

Books

  • “The Design of Everyday Things” by Don Norman - A foundational book on design principles and usability.
  • “Don't Make Me Think” by Steve Krug - A guide to web usability and user-centered design.
  • “About Face: The Essentials of Interaction Design” by Alan Cooper - Comprehensive insights into interaction design and user experience.

Online Courses

  • Coursera: Human-Computer Interaction - A course that covers the principles of HCI and user-centered design.
  • edX: Human-Computer Interaction - Offers insights into designing user-friendly interfaces.
  • Udacity: Human-Computer Interaction - Focuses on the design and evaluation of user interfaces.

Websites

  • Nielsen Norman Group - A leading organization in user experience research and training.
  • Smashing Magazine: Usability - Articles and resources on usability and user experience design.
  • Interaction Design Foundation - Offers a wealth of resources and courses on interaction design and HCI.
  • HTML - Offers a wealth of resources and courses on making an HTML interface

Tools

  • Sketch - A design tool for creating user interfaces and prototypes.
  • Figma - A collaborative interface design tool that allows for real-time collaboration.
  • Adobe XD - A vector-based tool for designing and prototyping user experiences.
